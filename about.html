Our Story

Founded in 2010 by beauty expert Emma Chen, Serenity Beauty Salon & Spa began as a small boutique salon with a vision to provide personalized, high-quality beauty experiences in a tranquil environment.

What started as a three-person team has now grown into a premier beauty destination with over 25 dedicated professionals, all committed to the same founding principles of quality, authenticity, and exceptional client care.

Throughout our journey, we've remained true to our core philosophy: beauty treatments should not just enhance your appearance but also nurture your overall wellbeing, leaving you feeling rejuvenated inside and out.
